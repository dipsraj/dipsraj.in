About Me

Hello, I'm Dipanjan Kundu, but you can call me Dipsraj.

By day, I'm a Senior Software Developer at Innoraft Solutions, where I build robust, scalable web solutions. My expertise lies in backend systems and Drupal, but my curiosity constantly pulls me toward the ever-evolving world of frontend development.

For me, life isn't just about code; it's about the journey. The thrill of hitting the open road on my bike, exploring winding mountain paths, and discovering new vistas is a core part of who I am. This love for exploration fuels my other passion: photography. I strive to capture the raw beauty of nature and the candid stories of people.

This blend of analytical problem-solving and creative expression defines my work, my art, and my life.
